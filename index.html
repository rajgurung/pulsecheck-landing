Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about PulseCheck

How is PulseCheck different from other monitoring tools?

+

PulseCheck focuses on simplicity and AI-powered insights. Unlike complex enterprise tools, we provide clear explanations of what went wrong and why, without overwhelming you with metrics. Perfect for indie developers who want monitoring that actually helps, not just alerts.

What types of services can I monitor?

+

Monitor anything with our universal health checks: web applications, APIs, background workers, cron jobs, job queues, databases, and more. Works with any tech stack - Python, Node.js, Ruby, Go, or whatever you're building with.

How does the AI explanation feature work?

+

When services fail or slow down, our AI analyzes patterns in your monitoring data and explains the likely cause in plain English. Instead of "HTTP 500", you get "Your database is overloaded during peak hours" with actionable next steps.

Is there a setup fee or long-term commitment?

+

No setup fees, no contracts. Start with our free plan and upgrade only when you need more projects or AI summaries. Cancel anytime. We believe monitoring should be accessible to every developer, not just enterprise teams.

How quickly will I know when something goes wrong?

+

Typically within 1-2 minutes. We use smart detection to avoid false alarms - you'll get notified when there's a real issue, not when a metric briefly spikes. Notifications via email, Slack, or webhook.

Can I export my monitoring data?

+

Absolutely. Your data belongs to you. Export monitoring history, downtime reports, and performance metrics anytime. No vendor lock-in - if PulseCheck isn't working for you, take your data and go.
